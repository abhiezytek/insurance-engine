ULIP
All the calculations are defined in the excel Bi and F&U. Possible components which you can check or validate:
1. Checking various type of charges – allocation charge, mortality charge (and additional risk benefit charge), policy admin charge, (FMC charge can be excluded – as it is calculated daily)
2. Surrender charge and surrender value
3. Matching various type of additions – loyalty addition, wealth booster, return of charges etc (as per policy terms and condition)
4. Validation of death benefit
5. Validation of boundary conditions as per F&U – like PT, PPT, Age etc
6. Validation of partial withdrawal benefit (as per F&U)
7. Policy benefits under paid-up status – (death benefit is proportionately reduced)
8. Validation of settlement benefit, free-look benefit

Century Income
1. Survival benefit – typically depends on plan option (3 options available).
a. Validation can be done for Guaranteed Income, Loyalty Income, Twin Income (available under option 3)
b. For reduced paid-up, the benefit would be reduced proportionately
c. You can validate calculation based as on date status (ie, past and future expected payouts based on the existing status continues)
i. You can also show, if the policy revives then how the future benefit would increase
2. Validation of Death Benefit, Maturity Benefit (current as well as future; and also under reduced paid-up status) 
3. Surrender benefit (SV) – Guaranteed SV, Special SV, (and also future SV as well based on current status, and if policy revive then how the amount would change etc)
4. Free-look cancellation value check
5. Calculation check of ‘Accumulation the income in Safe Box’ – (though the calculation is simple, however it will take more bandwidth to develop – so accordingly place this. However this could be valuable proposal from your side).
6. Calculation check – Income received on special date
7. Validation of policy loan eligibility as per T&C
8. Validation of additional benefit under staff / direct marketing / online channel – however this should be automatically part of your benefit calculation check
9. Validation of revival interest calculation and also any payout on revival (like applicable lump sump payment as per T&C)
